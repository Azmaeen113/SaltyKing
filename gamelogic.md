SALTY KINGDOM – Reward Points Model
The reward system should be designed around high engagement, replayability, and balanced progression pacing similar to successful Telegram mini-games.
The goal is:
Frequent small rewards
Medium event triggers every few rolls
Rare “hype” moments
Momentum-based progression
Controlled jackpot psychology
1. Core Philosophy
Salty Points = Primary in-game currency (off-chain)
$SALTY = Real token earned through gameplay
Maximum 3,000,000,000 (3 Billion) $SALTY will ever be distributed as rewards (hard cap)
Focus on daily habit, fairness, and long-term sustainability
Points act as score + future claimable value
No hyper-inflation — simple and transparent
POINTS to SALTY tokens converted later at 1000 Points to 1 SALTY

BOARD STRUCTURE (TOTAL 28 TILES)
COMMON REWARD TILES (18 TILES)  -  HISTORICAL STYLE TILES
 These are the base reward tiles for constant engagement.
6 Tiles → +2 Points
6 Tiles → +5 Points
4 Tiles → +10 Points
2 Tiles → +25 Points
Purpose:  Constant micro-rewards to avoid “empty” gameplay moments.

SPECIAL EVENT TILES (6 TILES)
A) SPIN CHARGE TILE (2)   -  HISTORICAL WHEEL IMAGE
Mechanic:
Each landing gives +1 Spin Charge
At 5 Charges → Unlock Spin Wheel
Purpose:  Creates fast reward cycles and increases retention.

B) BOOTY CHEST TILE (2)  -  HISTORICAL CHEST
Mechanic:
Each landing gives +1 Chest Key
At 3 Keys → Open BOOTY Chest
 Chest Rewards:  5,000 Points
Purpose:  Build anticipation and progression rewards.

C) MULTIPLIER TILE (2)    AS AN IMAGE  2X & 5X SHOWN ON TILE
Mechanic:  Applies multiplier to NEXT reward only.
Multiplier: 
2x
5x
Purpose:  Adds excitement and unpredictability.
